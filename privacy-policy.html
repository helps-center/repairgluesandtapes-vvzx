Privacy Policy

Okay, here's a rewrite of that privacy policy, aiming for a more human and less AI tone:

**1. What's This All About?**

Your privacy is seriously important to us. This explains how we gather, use, and keep your info safe when you're using our learning stuff online.

**2. What Info We Grab**

*   **For Students:**
    *   Your name, email, phone number, and how you log in.
    *   Optional: A profile pic, what you like to learn, and what courses you're in.
    *   How you're progressing, quiz scores, assignments, and certificates.
    *   Payment info if you're paying for courses.
*   **For Teachers:**
    *   Your name, what makes you qualified, a little about you, your email, and the stuff you upload.
    *   Course materials, videos, tests, and what students think.
*   **Techy Stuff:**
    *   Your IP address, what type of device you use, your operating system, browser, and how you use the app.
    *   When you access courses, finish lessons, and how long you spend learning.
    *   Cookies and info about how you learn.

**3. How We Use Your Info**

We use your info to:

*   Set up and run student and teacher accounts.
*   Give you learning stuff and see how you're doing.
*   Send out certificates when you finish a course.
*   Take payments and handle subscriptions.
*   Let you chat with teachers and other students (Q\&amp;A, comments, messages).
*   Watch how people learn and make things better.
*   Send reminders, updates, and suggest learning stuff.
*   Make sure everyone plays fair and follows the rules.

**4. Who We Share With**

We might share your info with:

*   **Teachers:** Your name, what you do in the course, and your work (just for the courses you're in).
*   **Payment Folks:** To keep payments safe.
*   **Analytics People:** To make the learning experience better.
*   **Certification Groups:** To double-check your certificates (if that applies).
*   **The Law:** If we have to, to follow the rules.

We won't sell your info to marketing companies.

**5. Cookies**

We use cookies to:

*   Keep you logged in.
*   Remember how far you've gotten in a course and what you like.
*   See how people are using the site.
*   Suggest courses you might like.

You can mess with cookie settings in your browser.

**6. How Long We Keep Your Stuff**

We keep your course info as long as your account is open, or as long as we need to for school or legal reasons. You can ask us to delete your account and info whenever you want.

**7. Your Rights**

*   You can see and change your personal or course info.
*   Ask us to delete your account and certificates.
*   Say no to email about deals or learning stuff.
*   Control your privacy settings and cookies.

**8. Keeping Your Info Safe**

We use encryption, secure servers, and controls to protect your learning info. But no system is perfect, so keep your login info safe!

**9. Kids' Privacy**

This site isn't for kids under 13 unless they're using it with a parent, guardian, or school. We don't knowingly grab info from kids without permission.

**10. Changes to This Policy**

We might update this policy as needed. We will put the changes on this page with the date they go live.
