About.

We're a bunch of fixers, thinkers, and teachers with one goal: helping folks fix and keep the stuff they love going. We think everything, big or small, deserves a second shot. With our great glues, tapes, and know-how, we give people the power to fix things themselves with confidence and creativity.

In a world that tosses things out too quickly, we're all about fixing instead. Gluing or taping isn't just a quick fix; it's about making things last longer, work better, and being kinder to the planet. By picking repair over replacing, our customers save cash and help cut down on trash.

What We Do

We want to be the go-to place for fixing anything, with awesome products and all the info you need. Our aim is to make fixing stuff easy and doable for everyone, from newbies to pros. We want folks to feel great every time they grab one of our glues or tapes, knowing they've got a product that's reliable, tough, and just right for the job.

We see fixing things as more than just making them work; it's a way to get creative and solve problems. Fixing a chair, patching a jacket, or mending a vase is a chance to learn, grow, and feel good about what you've done. That’s why we give you the tools, tips, and guides you need to turn everyday repairs into something fun and satisfying.

What We're Good At

We've got all sorts of glues and tapes for fixing anything and everything and they are made for any material or surface. We pick products that work great so you can get the job done right, no matter how simple or tough.

Types of Glues We Have

Super Glues: Fast fixes for plastics, metals, pottery, and glass.

Epoxy: Tough, waterproof glues for big repairs, factory jobs, and your projects.

Polyurethane Glues: Glues that expand to fill gaps, sticking wood, foam, concrete, and more.

Rubber and Fabric Glues: Bendy, washable glues for clothes, furniture, shoes, and bags.

Contact Cement: Great for laminates, rubber, leather, and sticking things fast.

Plastic Welding Glues: Special glues for mending hard plastics like PVC.

Hot Glue: Quick and easy for crafts, decorations, and small fixes.

Types of Tapes We Have

Duct Tape: Strong and useful for all sorts of fixes indoors, outdoors, or at work.

Electrical Tape: Stretchy tape that keeps wires safe.

Plumber’s Tape: Stops leaks at pipe joints.

Double-Sided Tape: For sticking things down, from crafts to carpets.

Waterproof Tape: Strong tape for leaks and outdoor quick fixes.

Silicone Tape: Sticks to itself to seal out air and water.

Aluminum Tape: Stands up to heat and water, great for ducts.

Fabric Tape: Tough and washable for tents, jackets, and bags.

No matter what you're fixing, we've got what you need to get it done, whether it has to be strong, bendy, waterproof, heat-proof, or safe for electronics.

What We Believe

We think fixing things is more than just a chore; it's cool, gives you skills, and helps the earth. Every time you fix something, you feel good, get smarter, and make less trash. By fixing instead of tossing, you make good choices that help things last longer and cut down on waste.

For us, knowing how is just as vital as having the stuff. We don't just sell glues and tapes. Our site gives you the info to pick the right product, get surfaces ready, use the stuff right, and fix any problems. From simple home fixes to big jobs, we make sure you know what you're doing and feel good about it.

Quality Stuff

Quality and trust are key for us. We try out every product to make sure it's safe, works well and lasts. We find products that work every time, whether it's for a small job or a huge one.

We also give advice and help for everything we sell. Our guides give you the steps to get things right and make the most of your fix. We're all about making sure your repairs are great, safe, and long-lasting.

Helping the Earth

We know fixing things helps the planet. By fixing instead of buying new, we use less stuff and make less trash. Every glue and tape is a small step toward living better by helping people and businesses cut down on waste while keeping things looking good and working well.

Where We're Going

We want to be the top place for all things related repair knowledge and products, getting people excited about fixing stuff and learning new things. Our goal is a world where fixing is normal, where tools are easy to get, and where everyone can fix things themselves at home, at work or anywhere.

By pushing a culture of fixing, we want to make the process of repairing things fun and worth it. We want you to feel ready to bring life back to the things you care about.

Why Pick Us?

Tons of Products: Glues and tapes for everything.

Expert Advice: Guides to make sure you get it right.

Good Quality: Products that are tested to be strong and safe.

Here for You: We know our stuff and want to help.

Eco-Friendly: Fixing instead of tossing to cut down on waste.

Always Improving: We add better products all the time.

Really, we're more than just a shop. We're here to help you fix things, solve problems, and live in a better way. Every glue, tape, and tip we give is meant to help you make things whole again, keep them going longer, and live smarter.
